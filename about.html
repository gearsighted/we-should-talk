About The Show

The We Should Talk Podcast is a collaboration between me and my amazing family and friends. I decided I wanted to learn more about the cool people in my life, and it turns out, they all felt the same way! Listen as we take turns interviewing friends and family in a frank, moderately unprofessional podcast focused on learning something new from the people in our lives.
