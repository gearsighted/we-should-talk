Episode 1: Cyndi

Cyndi Carnahan is a retired graphic designer and lifelong artist and craftsperson. She spends much of her time making beautiful and whimsical arts and crafts out of an amazingly diverse media, sometimes leaving the painstakingly crafted work in public places for strangers to find and take. Listen to my interview with my mom, in the first episode of the We Should Talk Podcast.
